I am currently a third-year PhD student in the [Colledge of Computer Science](https://cc.nankai.edu.cn/) at [Nankai University](https://www.nankai.edu.cn/), supervised by [Prof. Jufeng Yang](https://cv.nankai.edu.cn/) in Computer Vision Lab. Before that, I received my master’s degree from [Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University](https://www.fafu.edu.cn/) in 2022.
